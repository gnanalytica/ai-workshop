Welcome — the final stretch of the AI Workshop! Today marks the transition from learning to doing. You will clarify any lingering questions, dive into hands-on implementation, and kick off your project journey. By day's end, the AirDosa Project launches on social media, signaling the start of your real-world build.

**What you will achieve today:**
- Close knowledge gaps and build confidence
- Begin implementation of your project
- Generate momentum with your first social-media post

## ⏪ Pre-class

Before the session, review:
- Your project scope and technical stack (prepared from previous days)
- Any unanswered questions or blockers — write them down
- The AirDosa Project brief and talking points
- LinkedIn and Instagram best practices for tech/AI content

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Bring your laptop and development environment set up
- [ ] Identify your team members and communication channel
- [ ] Have a LinkedIn and Instagram account ready

## 🎥 In-class · live session

### Session 1: Open Doubt Clarification

- **Facilitator-led Q&A** — Address questions on AI concepts, implementation strategies, debugging, and project planning
- **Peer learning** — Teams share their approach; others offer feedback
- **Breakout sessions (optional)** — Deep dives on specific technical challenges
- **Resource sharing** — Curate links to documentation, code snippets, and templates

### Session 2: AirDosa Project — Social Media Launch

- **Content prep** — Finalize messaging and talking points
- **Review** brand guidelines and tone
- **Draft and edit** captions (LinkedIn + Instagram)
- **Scheduled posting** — LinkedIn and Instagram
- **Tag** mentors, instructors, and collaborators
