The final day. You are 48 hours into build mode. Today you will polish your product, fix and prepare to ship or demo with confidence. The finish line is in sight — sprint through it with everything you have got.
 
**Today's goal:** Ship a demo-ready product and present it to the cohort.
 
## ⏪ Pre-class
 
Before the session:
 
- [ ] Review the current state of your product (what is done, what is in progress)
- [ ] Identify important features for your final demo (must-have features)
- [ ] Plan testing: who tests what?
- [ ] Bonus points if everyone presents (we see you giving your best and your confidence to just show up to present)
- [ ] Confirm demo time and who speaks
## 🎥 In-class · live session
 
### Session 1: Sprint Standup & Prioritization (15 min)
 
- Brief standup from each team
- Focus on presentation and ask any questions if you have in-class
- Make sure you follow the instructions provided
## 📝 Post-class
 
By end of day:
 
- [ ] MVP complete and demo-ready
- [ ] All code committed, tested, and documented
- [ ] Demo script rehearsed and team confident
- [ ] Final social media post published (launch announcement)
### Post-workshop
 
- [ ] Archive repos and documentation
