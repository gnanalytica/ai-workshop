## 🎯 What we covered (one-liner each)

- **The data ladder.** Raw → Information → Intelligent → Decision.
- **How intelligent data is built.** Collection · cleaning · integration · enrichment · AI/ML layer.
- **Five questions to identify it.** Accurate? Has context? Answers a question? Model-ready? Triggers action?
- **Where it lives in the real world.** Education, healthcare, business, manufacturing, finance.
- **Why ethics stays in the loop.** Bad data scales bad decisions. Human oversight is non-negotiable.
