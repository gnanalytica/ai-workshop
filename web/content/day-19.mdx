> 🚩 **Milestone 2.** Graded checkpoint. By end of class, all four artifacts must be submitted. No idea-lock = no milestone credit.

Three weeks in. You have research, problem statements, and design-thinking artifacts. Today you commit. The goal isn't perfection — it's *legible enough* that a stranger can grasp what you're building in 90 seconds.

## 🎯 Today's objective

**Topic.** Capstone Milestone 2: Presentation, Pitch, Prototype + Mini Demo.

**By end of class you will have:**
1. A **5-slide deck**: problem, user, solution, prototype screenshot, ask.
2. A **60-second pitch** script you can deliver without notes.
3. A **clickable prototype** (Figma frames or a working stub page) — at least 3 screens connected.
4. A **90-second mini demo** screen recording walking through the prototype with voice-over.

### The 5-slide deck

1. **Problem** — one sentence + one statistic. No "in today's world."
2. **User** — one person, by name and role. *"Priya, 3rd-year CSE, fails 1 backlog/sem because reval forms are paper-only."*
3. **Solution** — one screenshot of your prototype + one sentence claim.
4. **Demo** — embed your 90-sec recording or a GIF that loops.
5. **Ask** — what you need from the cohort to ship Demo Day: testers, a co-founder, a domain expert.

### The 60-second pitch shape

- 0:00–0:15 — cold open with a story or stat. *"68 hours. That's how long my friend waited for a reval form last semester."*
- 0:15–0:35 — problem + user.
- 0:35–0:50 — your solution + the single most surprising thing about it.
- 0:50–1:00 — the ask. Make it concrete.

## 🧪 Lab: Ship the four

1. **Deck (15 min).** Open Gamma → "Generate from prompt" → paste your one-sentence problem. Edit the 5 slides ruthlessly. Export as PDF.
2. **Pitch (10 min).** Write the 60-second script *as you'd say it*. Read out loud. Cut 20%.
3. **Prototype (15 min).** In Figma: 3 frames minimum (home → core action → result). Connect them with prototype links. Share the link.
4. **Mini demo (10 min).** Loom or QuickTime. Open your prototype, talk through the 3 frames as if presenting. 90 seconds. Re-record once.

**Artifact.** Push all four to your capstone repo: `deck.pdf`, `pitch.md`, `prototype-url.txt`, `demo.mp4` (or Loom link).
