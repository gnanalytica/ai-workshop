Final 4 Days

Capstone: ship something real.

Teams of 2–3 pick a real problem — for a classmate, a local business, a campus process — and ship a working AI product in four days. Not a slide, not a mockup. A live thing you can hand over.

Campus placement assistant Local business AI receptionist Study-group tutor agent Research-paper analyzer Fest marketing automation Mess-menu voice agent
01

Scope & pick

Pressure-test an idea against "can a real person use this?" — not novelty, usefulness.

02

Build the ugly v0

Vibe-code the thinnest possible working path end-to-end. Working > beautiful.

03

Evaluate & harden

Write a tiny eval set. Fix the top 3 failure modes. Add citations, stop states, fallbacks.

04

Pitch & ship

Live demo + 3-slide story (problem, shape of solution, what's next). Hand it to a real user.
